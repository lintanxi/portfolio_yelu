Role

SOLO Project

Tools

Eko Studio, JavaScript
Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effect, Adobe Premiere

Key Words

Identity, China, One-child Policy, Master's Thesis

Overview

Lotus Baby is an Interactive Animation Game built through Eko Studio, on the language of JavaScript.

The game talks about the problems between two generations (parents born during 1960s and children born during 1990s) under China’s specific cultural context.

In this game, players are going to answer 10 questions under specific scenarios in order to create a PERFECT CHILD.
